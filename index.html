ABOUT US

The Best Holidays Start Here!

No 9 on Burger is situated at the heart of Polokwane, 3.8 km from Pietersburg Snake & Reptile. The Greenery shopping Center is 2km away while Savannah Mall is 4km away. The air-conditioned accommodation is 3 km from Polokwane Game Reserve, and guests can benefit from private parking available on site and free WiFi. The property is non-smoking and is set 1 km from Peter Mokaba Stadium. Polokwane Golf Club is 1 km away while Polokwane International Airport is 8 km from the property.

The house is composed of 2 air conditioned bedrooms that feature a flat screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen, and 2 bathrooms. Guests have access to unlimited WiFi and premium DSTV package. The property offers garden views with a beautiful chilling spot.
